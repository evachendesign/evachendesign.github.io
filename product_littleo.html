Little O is a voice assistance project. It is the first voice assistance on the Buick cars and the OnStar App ( the IoV tool app that controls the vehicle remotely ). This project improves connectivity among “People, Car, and Life.”

My Role

118

I designed the interaction for the 118 use cases according to the requirements.

UX / UI

I designed the mobile interfaces that assist the voice interaction.

VUI

I coordinated with the conversation design supplier and delivered the VUI.

Challenge

Design with Constraints

For this project, I designed with many constraints. I had to deal with user safety rules, cyber security checks, and data sharing authorization limitations.

Tight Schedule

Even though it was a tight schedule, I still managed to do the literature review for users’ problems. In addition, I coordinated with the engineers and data scientists to design a feasible prototype.

First Time VUI Design

It was my first time designing a voice assistant. In this project, I gained a more holistic understanding of the user scene of voice assistant products.

How to find all the path?

It was challenging to list all the possible user paths for the user cases like “Vehicle Data Query” and “Navigation”.

Step 2

In my approach to finding all the situations, I did secondary research on the Buick car models. And then confirmed the list with engineers to ensure the availability of the data.

Step 3

According to the data I found, I finished all the user flows and interfaces.
