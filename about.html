golf – passion beyond work

Breaking boundaries

Since 2021, golf has ignited my passion. It began with a challenge: surpassing my initial 70-yard drive with a 7-iron—a modest beginning that seemed to define my physical limits. The average for women amateurs stood at 80 yards, a benchmark I longed to beat. Through relentless practice at the driving range, I shattered my perceived barriers, advancing from 70 to 100, and eventually reaching beyond 120 yards. This journey not only extended my reach on the fairway but also significantly bolstered my confidence in many aspects of my life.

Poise in victory and adversity: resilience redefined

Golf, where victories and setbacks alike teach resilience and humility. Each shot on the course, a new beginning, challenges me to grow and adapt. It’s more than a game; it’s a life lesson in poise and perseverance, distilled into every shot.
